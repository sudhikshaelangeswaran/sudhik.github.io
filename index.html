Ceylon Tea

Ceylon tea is celebrated to be the finest in the world with the unique flavours, fragrance and freshness. The unique logo of Ceylon tea assures the quality of the product of the tea. Its hand picked by the exprienced pluckers and is mostly produced through orthodox and artisanal methods. It's one of the freshest tea available in the global market, expertly packed within the 3 weeks of harvessting, it preserves freshness, flavour, anbd aroma. A product that started as a diversification experiment in 1867 spanning just 19 arcs of land has today surpassed all geograpical borders to satisfy 19% of global demands. Sri lanka has become the worlds third largest tea exporter to the world.


Ceylon Tea Standards

The Sri lankan Tea industry maintains the highest quality in the world market and ISO 3720 is the minimum standard applied for the products. The country has the capability to produce the cleanest tea in the world in terms of minimum pesticides and residues.


Growers are constantly educated to practice Good Agricultural Practices(GAP). The processing/Manufacturing facilites that own export comapanies have local strandards (SLSI) and with international quality stanadrds such as ISO, HACCP and EU standards.

Herbal Tea

Herbal tea can tell the simple difference between a common tea variety and herbal tea with one sip. Tea is usually derived from the leaves of the "Camellia Sinesis"plant. Herbal tea is not your usual cup of tea. it is not a tea at all. it is an infusion or a blend of various leaves, flowers, fruits, or barks of a medicinal plant


There are different type of herbal tea

  2. Gotukola tea
